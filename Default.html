Helpful Tips

  • Tip 1 - Don't apply any product to the area being waxed(such as lotions, creams, makeup etc.) before coming in for service.
  • Tip 2 - It is not recommended to come in for a Brazilian during your menustral cycle due to sensitivity (Recommended for first timers).
  • Tip 3 - Take Ibuprofen 30 minutes before your scheduled appointment time. (Recommended for first timers.)
  • Tip 4 - The length of your hair must be the length of a grain of rice.
  • Tip 5 - Bring a fresh undergarment to change into after your appointment. Avoid tight fitting material that could irritate the skin.
